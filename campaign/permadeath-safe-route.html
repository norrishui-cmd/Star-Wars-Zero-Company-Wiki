Campaign Route

Permadeath-safe Route

A conservative route that values survivability and recovery windows.

RoutePlanning

Route Phases

Phase 1: Stabilize. Lock in sustain, frontline coverage, and one finisher.
Phase 2: Specialize. Upgrade the role that wins your hardest current fights.
Phase 3: Expand. Add optional objectives, bonds, and challenge rules once the core squad is reliable.

Prioritize

  • Mission timers
  • Injury recovery
  • Role gaps

Delay

  • Luxury damage
  • Unverified secret-class investment
  • Optional fights during low health windows
