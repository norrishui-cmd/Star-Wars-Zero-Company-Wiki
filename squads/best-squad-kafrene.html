Best Squad for Operation Kafrene

Recommended squad structure for Operation Kafrene, including roles, enemy threats, and safer alternatives.

Squad GuideLong-tail

Recommended Shell

Your first true field deployment out of the Den. A tutorialized op that teaches cover, flanking, Overwatch and the AP economy against light droid resistance.

  • Hawks - Force multiplier / flexible lead
  • Trick - Dependable frontline anchor
  • Kabb "Uppercut" - Frontline tank / bruiser
  • Luco Bronc - Long-range elimination

How to Play It

Open with scouting or cover setup, let the squad's safest role reveal enemy positions, then spend high-value actions only after a kill, objective step, or defensive reset is available.

Swap Rules

If the squad lacks sustain, reduce exposure. If it lacks burst, add a Sharpshooter or Gunslinger. If it lacks control, add Scout, Jedi, or suppression tools before hard missions.
