Special Gameplay Features

Verified entry points for Zero Company's distinctive tactical and campaign systems.

What Makes the Game Distinct

Zero Company combines a three-action-point tactical layer with a shared Advantage meter, persistent Injuries and a strategic base called The Den. Missions advance through HoloTable Cycles, while repeated deployments build bonds that unlock squad synergies.

Character creation is also part of the campaign structure: Hawks and recruited Operators can use eight confirmed species, and most Operators can change standard Specialization through Personnel. These links lead to the strongest current source-grounded guide for each system.
