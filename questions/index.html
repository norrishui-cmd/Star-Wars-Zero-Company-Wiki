Star Wars Zero Company FAQ

Direct answers to real pre-release questions. Every FAQ has its own URL, current source and explicit boundary between confirmed information and launch-day testing.

Release, Platforms & Editions

When does Star Wars Zero Company release?
Star Wars Zero Company releases on August 27, 2026.What time does Star Wars Zero Company release?
EA lists the release time as 3:00 PM UTC on August 27, 2026.What platforms is Star Wars Zero Company on?
The announced launch platforms are PC, PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S.Is Star Wars Zero Company coming to PS4?
No PS4 version has been announced.Is Star Wars Zero Company coming to Xbox One?
No Xbox One version has been announced.Is Star Wars Zero Company coming to Nintendo Switch?
Nintendo Switch and Switch 2 versions have not been announced.Where can you buy Star Wars Zero Company on PC?
The PC version is sold through the EA app, Steam and Epic Games Store.How much does Star Wars Zero Company cost?
The US storefront price for the standard edition is $49.99 before applicable taxes.How much is the Zero Company Deluxe Edition?
The US storefront price for the Deluxe Edition is $59.99 before applicable taxes.Should you preorder Star Wars Zero Company?
Preordering is optional; the announced incentive is the Crystalline Astromech Cosmetic Pack.What is the Star Wars Zero Company preorder bonus?
Preorders include the Crystalline Astromech Cosmetic Pack.What is included in the Zero Company Deluxe Edition?
The Deluxe Edition adds Clone Wars-inspired cosmetic items.How many languages does Star Wars Zero Company support?
The official stores list 11 interface and subtitle languages.Does Star Wars Zero Company have German voice acting?
Yes. German is listed with full audio, interface and subtitles.Does Star Wars Zero Company have Japanese voice acting?
Japanese interface and subtitles are listed, but Japanese full audio is not.

Game Modes & Tactical Systems

Is Star Wars Zero Company single-player?
Yes. EA describes Zero Company as a single-player turn-based tactics game.Does Star Wars Zero Company have multiplayer?
No multiplayer mode appears in the announced feature set.Does Star Wars Zero Company have co-op?
Co-op has not been announced.Can Star Wars Zero Company be played offline?
EA lists the PC online connection requirement as N/A, but platform-level offline behavior should be verified at launch.How many action points does an Operator get per turn?
Each Operator receives three action points per turn.What can action points be used for?
Action points pay for movement, attacks and abilities during an Operator turn.What is Advantage in Star Wars Zero Company?
Advantage is a shared squad resource generated by attacking enemies.What is the maximum Advantage?
The Advantage meter is capped at 10.Should you save Advantage?
Save it only when a later squad action is more valuable than the available spend now.How do Injuries work in Zero Company?
Operators can carry Injuries from tactical combat into the campaign layer.How many Injuries cause permanent death?
On standard difficulty, the third Injury permanently kills the Operator.Can story characters die permanently?
Yes. EA says permanent death can affect authored story Operators as well as custom recruits.Does Zero Company have limited saves?
EA says higher difficulties include limited saves.What are Mission Cycles?
Mission Cycles are the HoloTable structure used to present and advance campaign opportunities.Are the tactical maps handcrafted?
Yes. EA describes the maps as handcrafted.Are missions randomized in Zero Company?
Missions use handcrafted maps with randomized elements rather than fully procedural maps.What mission types are confirmed?
EA mentions tactical operations, investigations and other missions.

Specializations & Operators

How many standard Specializations are there?
EA has confirmed eight standard Specializations.What are the eight standard Specializations?
They are Assault, Gunslinger, Heavy, Medic, Scoundrel, Scout, Sharpshooter and Soldier.Can Operators change Specialization?
Most Operators can change their Specialization through the Personnel facility.How many abilities does a standard Specialization have?
Each standard Specialization has one Ultimate, one Standard Ability and one Passive.What are Focus Points used for?
Focus Points improve Talents, Abilities and Passives.Is Hawks customizable?
Yes. Players can customize Hawks’ appearance and combat Specialization.Can you create custom Operators?
Yes. The Recruitment facility allows players to create custom squad members.Can you use only authored Operators?
EA confirms both authored and custom Operators, but it has not stated that custom recruitment is mandatory.What do squad bonds do?
Deploying Operators together improves bonds and unlocks combat synergies.Which Zero Company Operatives are officially profiled?
EA has profiled Hawks, Trick, Tel-Rea, Cly, Luco, Jae and Kabb.Who is Trick in Zero Company?
Trick is a hardened Clone Trooper and a member of Zero Company.Who is Tel’Rea Vokoss?
Tel’Rea Vokoss is a Jedi Padawan who joins Zero Company.Who is Cly Kullervo?
Cly Kullervo is a Mandalorian warrior associated with the ancient Clan Verminoth.Who is Luco Bronc?
Luco Bronc is an Umbaran sniper in Zero Company.

The Den, Story & Locations

What is The Den?
The Den is Zero Company’s home base between missions.What facilities are in The Den?
EA describes Command Center, HoloTable, Recruitment, Personnel, Armory, Upgrade, Medbay, Customization and Memorial functions.Who is Kundri Fathom?
Kundri Fathom is the enigmatic leader Zero Company is assigned to track and stop.Where does Star Wars Zero Company take place?
The story is set during the twilight of the Clone Wars and spans multiple galactic locations.
