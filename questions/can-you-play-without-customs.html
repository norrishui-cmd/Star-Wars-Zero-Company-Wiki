Decision FAQ

Can you play without custom recruits?

Named-only roster planning and backup risk.

Short Answer

You can lean on authored operatives, but custom backups reduce campaign failure pressure.

Decision Tree

If survival is uncertain: choose the safer role or delay the risk.
If the objective timer is tight: value mobility and AP efficiency over full clears.
If the squad has a missing role: fix the missing role before optimizing damage.

Common Trap

The most common mistake is treating the question as a yes/no rule. In Zero Company, the right answer changes with map pressure, injury status, and available squad roles.
