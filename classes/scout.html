Quick Answer

Uses planning and timing to create opportunities for the whole squad.

How to Think About Scout

  • Gather or exploit information before high-cost attacks are committed.
  • Plan around mission objectives and enemy movement, not only personal damage.
  • Use Scout-created openings with finishers that can convert information into progress.

These are decision principles derived from the officially stated role. They are not a final build, tier ranking or substitute for launch testing.

Specialization and Progression

EA says most Operators use one of eight standard Specializations. Each standard Specialization contains an Ultimate, a Standard Ability and a Passive unique to that Specialization. In the Personnel facility, most Operators can change Specialization, and Focus Points can improve Talents, Abilities and Passives.
