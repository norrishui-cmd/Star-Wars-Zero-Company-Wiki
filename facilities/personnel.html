The Den facility

Personnel Facility

Manage stats, Focus Trees, Specializations and Bonds between Operators.

Officially confirmedChecked July 18, 2026

What Does the Personnel Facility Do?

The Personnel facility displays combat stats, Soldier Bonds and Focus Trees. It is the central progression screen for individual Operators and pair relationships.

Confirmed Functions

  • Focus Points improve Talents, Abilities and Passives.
  • Most Operators can change Specialization from this facility.
  • Bond levels can grant permanent Cross Training combat bonuses.
  • Maximum Bonds unlock a special Passive-based Cross Training benefit.

Launch Verification Boundary

Exact Focus Point costs, Bond thresholds and stat bonuses await launch-build verification.

This page deliberately avoids invented costs, rankings and upgrade orders.

Why This Facility Matters

The Den connects tactical combat with long-term campaign decisions. Using the Personnel Facility changes what Zero Company can attempt next, how prepared the roster is and which resources remain available. The practical guide will expand after the shipped economy and interface can be tested.
