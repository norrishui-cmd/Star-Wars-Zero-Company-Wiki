The Den Facilities

Verified guides to the HoloTable, Recruitment, Personnel, Armory, Upgrade, Medbay, Customization and Memorial facilities.

Officially confirmedChecked July 18, 2026

Verified Directory

HoloTable
The HoloTable is the mission-planning center for Tactical Missions, Operations and Zone Influence.Recruitment Facility
Recruit Custom Operators with Credits or prepare your own candidate pool.Personnel Facility
Manage stats, Focus Trees, Specializations and Bonds between Operators.Armory
Equip weapons, Weapon Modifications and combat Utilities at the Den.Upgrade Facility
Build Den infrastructure and roster-wide improvements over campaign Cycles.Medbay
Treat injured Operators with standard beds or immediate Bacta Tank recovery.Customization Facility
Change Operator appearance, outfits and supported species at the Den.Memorial
Review fallen Operators and the service records they left behind.The Den Command Center
The Den is Zero Company's base on the Ring of Kafrene and the hub connecting every campaign system.
