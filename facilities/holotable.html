The Den facility

HoloTable

The HoloTable is the mission-planning center for Tactical Missions, Operations and Zone Influence.

Officially confirmedChecked July 18, 2026

What Does the HoloTable Do?

At the HoloTable, players view the available Operations and Tactical Missions for the current Cycle and track Zero Company's Influence.

Confirmed Functions

  • Operations are non-combat assignments that can gather information, loot or Credits.
  • Operations cost Intel; Intel is earned through Tactical Missions.
  • Tactical Missions advance the game to the next Cycle after completion.
  • Some activities expire, so the HoloTable supports time-sensitive planning.

Launch Verification Boundary

Exact mission pools, Cycle length and every reward table remain launch-verification items.

This page deliberately avoids invented costs, rankings and upgrade orders.

Why This Facility Matters

The Den connects tactical combat with long-term campaign decisions. Using the HoloTable changes what Zero Company can attempt next, how prepared the roster is and which resources remain available. The practical guide will expand after the shipped economy and interface can be tested.
