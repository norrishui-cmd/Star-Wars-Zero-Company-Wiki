Custom Operator Species

EA has confirmed eight species for Hawks and recruited Operators: Devaronian, Human, Neimoidian, Ovissian, Togruta, Twi'lek, Weequay and Zabrak.

Confirmed Creator Data

The June 2026 press release connects these species with customizable voices, outfits, appearances, Specializations and Talents. It does not publish racial bonuses or species-locked roles.
