Permadeath Safety vs Speed Objective

Safety preserves campaign value; speed preserves expiring rewards.

Versus Intent

Permadeath Safety

Pick Permadeath Safety when the mission rewards its main strength and your squad can cover its weakness.

A

Best when the map asks for proactive control.

Speed Objective

Pick Speed Objective when the squad needs consistency, recovery, or a safer fallback plan.

B+

Best when campaign risk matters more than tempo.

Recommendation

Safety preserves campaign value; speed preserves expiring rewards. The best choice is the one that fixes your squad's current missing role rather than the option with the flashier ceiling.
