Launch Week Verification Plan

The maintenance plan for replacing pre-launch estimates with verified content during release week.

ConfirmedPreview-labeled

Before Launch

  • Keep estimated tiers marked as pre-launch.
  • Use confirmed official pages for release/platform/edition data.
  • Keep scenario guides generic when mission details are unknown.

After Launch

  • Verify class names, abilities, AP costs, enemy names, mission titles and rewards.
  • Split spoiler-safe and spoiler-full walkthroughs.
  • Refresh sitemap and update log after each major content pass.
