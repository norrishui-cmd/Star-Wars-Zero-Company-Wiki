What Is Star Wars Zero Company?

A source-anchored overview of the game as a single-player Clone Wars-era turn-based tactics title from Bit Reactor and Respawn.

ConfirmedPreview-labeled

Confirmed Summary

  • Single-player turn-based tactics game set in the Clone Wars era.
  • Players lead Hawks and Zero Company through tactical operations, investigations and story missions.
  • Development is led by Bit Reactor with Respawn and Lucasfilm Games involvement, published by EA.

Why It Matters for Guides

  • The site should prioritize tactical decision-making, squad management and campaign consequences rather than only lore summaries.
  • Because the game blends tactics and RPG-style story, both mechanics pages and relationship/consequence pages matter.
