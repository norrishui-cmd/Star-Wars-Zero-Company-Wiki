Operating Rules

Rule 1

Evidence before indexation

A generated URL remains noindex until it contains a concrete, supportable answer.

Rule 2

Unknowns are labeled

Pre-release facts are kept separate from launch values, builds and rankings.

Rule 3

Player intent first

Every indexable page targets one distinct question, entity or task.
