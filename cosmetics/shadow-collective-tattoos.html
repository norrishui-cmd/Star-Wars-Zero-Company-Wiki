What Is Included

Includes Pyke Syndicate, Hutt Cartel, Death Watch, Black Sun Syndicate and Crimson Dawn tattoos. EA and its support guide categorize this content as cosmetic. No damage increase, exclusive combat ability or campaign mission is advertised as part of this item.

That distinction matters for edition comparisons: this entry records appearance content and acquisition, not a power advantage. Regional store wording and prices can vary while the named contents remain the useful comparison point.

Current Status and Verification

The item is listed in the current preorder or Deluxe Edition material. It becomes usable when Star Wars Zero Company launches on August 27, 2026 for an account that meets the relevant purchase condition.

In-game menu location, compatible body types, recolor controls and final preview options will be added only after launch testing.
