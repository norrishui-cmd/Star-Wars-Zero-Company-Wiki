Content Architecture

Content Map and Gap Audit

This page defines what a high-quality tactics RPG guide site should contain, then audits this wiki against that structure. It is designed to prevent thin pages and unsupported claims.

Editorial ToolGap Audit

What This Site Should Be

A Star Wars Zero Company guide site should work like a tactical command desk: official facts establish trust, systems pages explain the rules, planner pages help decisions, and launch-week updates replace preview assumptions with verified data.

Do More Of This

  • Source-labeled confirmed information.
  • Decision trees and checklists.
  • Scenario pages when mission names are unknown.
  • Post-launch verification notes.

Avoid This

  • Fake mission names presented as confirmed.
  • Final tier scores before balance is known.
  • Unverified prices by region.
  • Unsupported screenshots interpreted as hard stats.
