Star Wars Zero Company Release Date and Time

Star Wars Zero Company releases on August 27, 2026. EA's PC store lists the unlock time as 3:00 PM UTC (8:00 AM PDT / 11:00 AM EDT).

Date Confirmed3 PM UTC on PC

Quick Answer

  • Date: Thursday, August 27, 2026
  • EA app time: 3:00 PM UTC
  • Platforms: PC, PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S
  • Mode: single-player turn-based tactics

Is There Early Access?

EA has announced Standard and Deluxe editions, but the official edition descriptions do not currently promise an early-access period. The Deluxe Edition is presented as a cosmetic bundle, not an earlier release. Unless EA updates the offer, plan around the August 27 launch.

Console Timing and Preload

The exact console unlock time and preload window may be displayed by the PlayStation or Xbox store for each region. EA's buy page provides a precise 3 PM UTC time for the EA app; this site does not assume that every console region uses the same clock. Check your purchased store listing close to launch.
