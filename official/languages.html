Supported Languages

Eleven interface and subtitle languages are confirmed. Full audio is available in English and German.

Full language list

English, French, Italian, German, Spanish (Spain), Japanese, Korean, Polish, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese and Portuguese (Brazil).

Language featureAvailability
Interface and subtitlesAll 11 listed languages
Full audioEnglish and German

EA lists the eleven supported languages on its purchase page. Steam and the Epic Games Store provide the more detailed separation between text and full audio.
