Star Wars Zero Company Platforms and Stores

The confirmed platforms are PC, PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S. PC players can buy through the EA app, Steam or Epic Games Store.

Is It on Steam?

Yes. EA links directly to Steam as one of the PC preorder storefronts. The EA app and Epic Games Store are also official PC purchase options.

Is It on Switch 2?

No Switch or Switch 2 release has been announced. That is a statement about the current official platform list, not proof that a later port can never happen.

Cross-save and Cross-progression

EA describes Zero Company as a single-player game, but has not published a cross-save or cross-progression feature announcement. Do not choose a store expecting transferable saves unless the relevant store or EA later confirms it.
