Gunslinger vs Commando Droid

A narrow tactical answer for players asking whether Gunslinger can handle Commando Droid safely.

Threat AMulti-target chainerGood Fit

Short Answer

Gunslinger can contribute strongly against Commando Droid. The key is to respect the enemy resistance: Evasive against single long-range shots while moving.

AP Plan

  1. Use the first action to improve position, strip cover, reveal, or prepare the shot.
  2. Commit damage only after the weak point is available: Crowd control and Overwatch — it wants to close distance; deny the approach.
  3. Keep one defensive or extraction option if the kill is not guaranteed.

Best Support

Pair Gunslinger with Commander AP refresh, Medic sustain, or a frontline bodyguard when the enemy can punish missed shots or exposed positioning.
