Mandalorian Warrior vs B2 Super Battle Droid

A narrow tactical answer for players asking whether Mandalorian Warrior can handle B2 Super Battle Droid safely.

Threat BMobile burst · exotic (Cly)Good Fit

Short Answer

Mandalorian Warrior can contribute strongly against B2 Super Battle Droid. The key is to respect the enemy resistance: Light blaster fire from the front (chip damage).

AP Plan

  1. Use the first action to improve position, strip cover, reveal, or prepare the shot.
  2. Commit damage only after the weak point is available: Flanking, explosives, Heavy fire —armor falls off fast once exposed.
  3. Keep one defensive or extraction option if the kill is not guaranteed.

Best Support

Pair Mandalorian Warrior with Commander AP refresh, Medic sustain, or a frontline bodyguard when the enemy can punish missed shots or exposed positioning.
