Operation Walkthrough

Ryloth Extraction

An optional smash-and-grab on Ryloth. Generous Advantage economy makes it a clean playground for an aggressive, mobility-first squad —but it vanishes from the galaxy map if you wait too long.

Side OpRylothSide op · time-limited

Objectives

  • Raid the supply cache
  • Exfiltrate before reinforcements arrive

Walkthrough

  1. Hit fast. Jetpack Cly onto the cache roof turn one for an immediate flank.
  2. Snowball with Jae. Identify Weakness into a Gunslinger chain clears the courtyard quickly.
  3. Don't linger. Grab the cache and exfil —reinforcements scale the longer you stay.
