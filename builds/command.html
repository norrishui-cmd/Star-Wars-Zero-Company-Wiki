Loadout Guide

Command Multiplier

Hawks doesn't out-damage the squad — he doubles its best member. Build around feeding AP refreshes to your highest-output operative and keeping Hawks safe enough to do it every turn.

Tier SAction-economy engineFor Hawks (Soldier base)

Focus Priority

  • Field Command → Press the Advantage → No One Left: cheaper, longer-range refreshes that also buff damage, with a free Rally as insurance.

Weapon & Secondary

Weapon: Rifle — flexible mid-range so Hawks contributes between refreshes.

Secondary: Scout or Medic — utility that keeps him useful when Advantage is dry.

Squad Synergy

Pair with a Gunslinger or Sharpshooter: refresh their AP after a kill and chain a second elimination. The single strongest tempo play in the game.

Strengths

  • Turns one carry into two turns of output
  • Flexible — fits any composition
  • Free Rally mitigates permadeath risk

Weaknesses

  • Advantage-hungry; competes with ultimates
  • Low solo damage ceiling
  • Mandatory in story missions — can't bench him
