Loadout Guide

Saber Control

A reset button on a clustered enemy line. Dive in, Shockwave the cluster to scatter and damage it, then clean up with deflection keeping her alive in the open.

Tier SDisruption / meleeFor Tel'Rea Vokoss (Jedi Padawan)

Focus Priority

  • Saber Throw →Deflection →Force Surge: ranged opener, survivability while she has AP, then Shockwave that also strips enemy AP.

Weapon & Secondary

Weapon: Lightsaber —melee; she's an entry tool, not a sniper.

Secondary: Locked exotic kit; lean into mobility talents.

Squad Synergy

Best with a Sharpshooter to delete the scattered survivors and a Medic to top her up after the dive.

Strengths

  • Best AoE crowd control in the game
  • High mobility entry
  • Shockwave denies enemy actions

Weaknesses

  • Fragile in the open without Deflection up
  • Advantage-hungry ultimate
  • Single operative —not a repeatable archetype
