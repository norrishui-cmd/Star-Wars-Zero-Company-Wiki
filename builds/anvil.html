Loadout Guide

Anvil Heavy

The wall the rest of the squad hides behind. Taunt enemies onto Kabb, eat the damage with plating, and let your damage dealers work from safety. Melee finishers refund AP for repeat swings.

Tier AFrontline tankFor Kabb / custom Heavy

Focus Priority

  • Plating → Bulwark → Knockout: stack damage reduction, force enemies to attack you, then snowball melee kills.

Weapon & Secondary

Weapon: Repeater — area suppression between melee engages.

Secondary: Soldier — extra durability and flexible mid-range tools.

Squad Synergy

Requires a Medic behind him (sustained anvil) and pairs with Gunslinger finishers on the enemies he's pinned.

Strengths

  • Soaks enemy focus, protects the squad
  • Controls enemy targeting via taunt
  • Strong melee burst on staggered targets

Weaknesses

  • Low mobility — slow to reposition
  • Limited range output
  • Falls behind without healing support
