Pairing Guide

Trick + Custom · Gunslinger

A focused synergy page for building around Trick as Dependable frontline anchor and Custom · Gunslinger as Multi-target / chain finisher.

SoldierGunslinger

Why the Pair Works

Trick brings dependable frontline anchor, while Custom · Gunslinger covers multi-target / chain finisher. The pair should be played around turn economy: one unit creates the opening, the other converts it into damage, safety, or objective progress.

Risk Notes

This pair is best when deployed repeatedly so bond progress can turn a simple role pairing into a reliable tactical bonus.

Best Third and Fourth Slots

  • Bring a Medic or Astromech if either unit must expose themselves.
  • Add a Sharpshooter or Gunslinger if the pair creates openings but lacks finishing damage.
  • Add a Heavy if enemies can rush the backline.
