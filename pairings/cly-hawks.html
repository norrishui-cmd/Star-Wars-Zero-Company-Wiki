Pairing Guide

Cly Kullervo + Hawks

A focused synergy page for building around Cly Kullervo as Vertical flanker / mobile burst and Hawks as Force multiplier / flexible lead.

Mandalorian Warrior · exoticCommander · Soldier base

Why the Pair Works

Cly Kullervo brings vertical flanker / mobile burst, while Hawks covers force multiplier / flexible lead. The pair should be played around turn economy: one unit creates the opening, the other converts it into damage, safety, or objective progress.

Risk Notes

This pair is best when deployed repeatedly so bond progress can turn a simple role pairing into a reliable tactical bonus.

Best Third and Fourth Slots

  • Bring a Medic or Astromech if either unit must expose themselves.
  • Add a Sharpshooter or Gunslinger if the pair creates openings but lacks finishing damage.
  • Add a Heavy if enemies can rush the backline.
