Pairing Guide

Kabb "Uppercut" + Trick

A focused synergy page for building around Kabb "Uppercut" as Frontline tank / bruiser and Trick as Dependable frontline anchor.

Heavy / brawlerSoldier

Why the Pair Works

Kabb "Uppercut" brings frontline tank / bruiser, while Trick covers dependable frontline anchor. The pair should be played around turn economy: one unit creates the opening, the other converts it into damage, safety, or objective progress.

Risk Notes

This pair is best when deployed repeatedly so bond progress can turn a simple role pairing into a reliable tactical bonus.

Best Third and Fourth Slots

  • Bring a Medic or Astromech if either unit must expose themselves.
  • Add a Sharpshooter or Gunslinger if the pair creates openings but lacks finishing damage.
  • Add a Heavy if enemies can rush the backline.
